About 0M_P

Sound Producer / Singer / VOCALOID Producer / Writer

Started activities in 2017. VOCALOID songs are mainly sung by Hatsune Miku. Made major debut in 2022 from Polydor Records, a label under Universal Music Japan. Major works include "You Need", "natural love", and "Drama of Play".
